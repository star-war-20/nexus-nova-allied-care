About Nexus Nova

Governance-led care that keeps people safe and connected

Nexus Nova Allied Care Services Ltd is a UK care organisation delivering integrated children's services and adult social care. We are committed to safe transitions, consistent relationships and measurable outcomes.

Company Overview

Our services are designed to provide stability, safeguarding and independence for children, young people and adults.
